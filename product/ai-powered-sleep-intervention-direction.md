## Strategy Shorthand

Internally, Orisen may be described as building toward a hardware layer for AI-powered sleep intervention.

Product-source-of-truth docs should usually use the more precise wording:

> a non-wearable bedroom hardware foundation for software-defined sleep interventions, including possible future AI-assisted layers

This means Orisen's hardware foundation may eventually let software-defined interventions use bedroom context and act through audio, light, timing, wake-completion behavior, and possibly voice.

## Non-Negotiable Boundaries

This direction does not change the current product truth:

- Orisen is currently a non-contact sleep intervention device for people who struggle to wake up.
- Wake completion remains the first wedge and strongest current evidence.
- Reliability comes before intelligence.
- The engineering MVP does not require AI agents, voice, validated vital-sign sensing, validated sleep-stage sensing, or autonomous intervention.
- AI agents, voice intervention, vital-sign sensing, sleep-stage sensing, and autonomous intervention remain future-facing unless validated.
- Orisen should not be positioned as an AI-agent company at the expense of the wake-up wedge.
