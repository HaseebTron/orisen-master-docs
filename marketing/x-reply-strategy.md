## Purpose

This file will define Orisen's reply strategy for the founder's personal X account.

Replies are treated as a separate growth and discovery workflow from owned posts because early low-follower accounts often need to earn profile visits through existing conversations.

This file should eventually define:

- who to reply to
- what conversations to look for
- what search terms to use
- how to reply without sounding spammy
- when to mention Orisen
- when to invite DMs
- when not to reply
- how replies feed customer discovery, post ideas, and waitlist growth
