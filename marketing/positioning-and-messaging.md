## Default positioning

Orisen is a non-contact sleep intervention device for people who struggle to wake up.

It helps make waking up more reliable by using presence detection to check whether the user actually got out of bed, and it is being built toward less brutal mornings through gradual audio, light, and future sensor-informed intervention.

## Simple explanation

Orisen is not just another alarm clock.

A normal alarm asks whether the alarm went off.

Orisen asks whether you actually got out of bed.

## Main messaging wedge

Lead with the painful wake-up problem:

- Oversleeping
- Sleeping through alarms
- Turning alarms off and going back to bed
- Waking up brutally groggy
- Feeling like normal alarms and willpower do not work

## What to emphasize

Emphasize:

- Built for people who struggle to wake up
- Presence-based wake completion
- Local alarm reliability
- Re-trigger behavior if the user returns to bed
- Gradual audio and light wake-up
- Future sensor-informed wake intervention
- Not a wearable
- Not a passive sleep tracker

## Safe claims

Generally safe claims:

- Orisen is a non-contact sleep intervention device.
- Orisen is built for people who struggle to wake up.
- Orisen is designed to make waking up more reliable.
- Orisen uses presence detection to help determine whether the user actually got out of bed.
- Orisen is not primarily a sleep tracker.
- Orisen is not a wearable.

## Careful claims

Use careful wording for:

- Helping users get out of bed
- Reducing the chance of dismissing the alarm and going back to sleep
- Making mornings feel less brutal
- Reducing grogginess
- Sensor-informed wake intervention
- Sleep-phase-aware wake-up behavior

Prefer wording like:

- “designed to”
- “aims to”
- “helps”
- “working toward”
- “being built toward”

## Claims to avoid for now

Avoid:

- “Eliminates grogginess”
- “Wakes you in the perfect sleep stage”
- “Controls your sleep stages”
- “Clinically proven”
- “Clinically improves sleep inertia”
- “Guarantees better sleep”
- “Accurately detects sleep stages”
- “Manipulates biology in real time”
- “Treats sleep disorders”
- “Medical-grade sleep intervention”

## Messaging to avoid

Avoid positioning Orisen as:

- A generic smart alarm clock
- A sleep tracker
- A wellness dashboard
- A medical device
- A gadget for casual sleep optimization
- A product only for people who like data and graphs
