This evidence may help Orisen understand:

- how common alarm dependence, snoozing, and oversleeping may be

- which wake-up problems are closest to Orisen's first wedge

## What this evidence does not prove

This evidence does not prove:

- Orisen reduces grogginess
- Orisen reduces sleep inertia
- Orisen improves wake-up outcomes in real users
- broad-market demand for Orisen
- willingness to pay for Orisen
- clinical efficacy
