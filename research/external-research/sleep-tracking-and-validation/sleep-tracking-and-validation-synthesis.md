This evidence may help Orisen understand:

- how sleep tracking devices are validated

## What this evidence does not prove

This evidence does not prove:

- Orisen can accurately detect sleep stages
- Orisen can replace PSG
- Orisen can make clinical sleep claims
- Orisen can reliably infer sleep state in real bedrooms
- Orisen can safely claim sleep-phase-aware performance before testing
