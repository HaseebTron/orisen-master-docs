## What this evidence may help with

This evidence may help Orisen understand:

- what users already try
- what existing products fail to solve
- what buyers already pay for
- how to position Orisen against alarms, apps, wearables, and sunrise lamps
- what product gaps matter most to users

## What this evidence does not prove

This evidence does not prove:

- Orisen will beat existing alternatives
- Orisen has product-market fit
- Orisen has validated willingness to pay
- Orisen solves the failure modes better than competitors

## Claim-safety notes

Competitor evidence should be used for positioning and product learning, not proof of Orisen efficacy.

Be careful not to cherry-pick negative competitor reviews as if they represent all users.
