## What this evidence may help with

This evidence may help Orisen understand:

- how common difficulty falling asleep may be
- what users already try before bed
- which future bedtime-support features are plausible
- how circadian light, sound, and routine design may fit the long-term product direction

## What this evidence does not prove

This evidence does not prove:

- Orisen improves sleep onset
- Orisen treats insomnia
- Orisen improves sleep quality
- bedtime features should be prioritized over wake-up reliability
- broad demand for Orisen's first product

## Claim-safety notes

Going-to-sleep research should be treated as future-roadmap support unless Orisen-specific testing shows direct user value.
