This evidence may help Orisen understand:

## What this evidence does not prove

This evidence does not prove:

- Orisen improves sleep quality
- Orisen changes sleep stages
- Orisen reduces sleep disruption
- Orisen can accurately infer sleep stage in the bedroom
- Orisen has clinical efficacy

## Claim-safety notes

It should not be converted into customer-facing claims without Orisen-specific validation.
