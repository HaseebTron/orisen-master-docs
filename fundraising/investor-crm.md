# Investor CRM

## Purpose

This file tracks people who may help Orisen with investor conversations, warm intros, startup advice, sleep/hardware connections, or fundraising-related introductions.

Use this as a lightweight CRM that is easy for a human to scan and easy for ChatGPT to search/update.

## Status key

- `Not reached out`: no message sent yet
- `Reached out`: message sent, response not yet recorded
- `Responded`: they replied
- `Intro possible`: they may be able to make an intro
- `Intro made`: intro has been made
- `Follow-up needed`: needs another message
- `Closed / not useful`: no obvious next step

## Priority key

- `High`: likely direct investor access, strong warm intro, or strong strategic relevance
- `Medium`: may know investors or relevant founders/operators
- `Low`: possible but uncertain relevance
- `Unknown`: needs qualification
