# Outreach Email Accounts

## Purpose

This file defines which email account should be used for Orisen fundraising and investor-related outreach.

The goal is to keep outreach professional, credible, and easy to manage.

## Available email accounts

| Email | Type | Best Use | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| `haseeb@orisen.ai` | Company email | Primary investor outreach, VC outreach, angel outreach, founder/operator outreach, formal follow-ups | Best default for fundraising because it looks company-specific and professional. |
| `abdul.haseeb@uwaterloo.ca` | University email | Warm intros where University of Waterloo credibility is especially relevant, Waterloo alumni, professors, Velocity/UWaterloo ecosystem contacts | Useful when the Waterloo connection is part of the relationship or credibility signal. |
| `ahaseeb2002@gmail.com` | Personal email | Existing personal relationships, informal contacts, backup account | Not ideal as the default investor outreach email because it looks less company-specific. |

## Default rule

Use `haseeb@orisen.ai` as the default email for investor and fundraising outreach.

Reasons:

- It reinforces that Orisen is a real company, not just a student project.
- It keeps investor communication separate from personal email.
- It looks more professional in cold or semi-warm investor outreach.
- It makes future handoff, CRM tracking, and follow-up cleaner.

## When to use the Waterloo email

Use `abdul.haseeb@uwaterloo.ca` when the Waterloo association meaningfully helps the outreach.

Good cases:

- Contacting Waterloo alumni
- Contacting Velocity-related people
- Contacting professors or university-linked advisors
- Asking for intros through university or alumni networks
- When the recipient may trust the email more because of the Waterloo domain

Suggested pattern:

- Send from `abdul.haseeb@uwaterloo.ca` only when the Waterloo context is part of the reason they should open/respond.
- In the message, still mention Orisen clearly and link to the company site/deck.
- If the conversation becomes investor-facing or formal, move the thread to `haseeb@orisen.ai` or CC it.

## When to use the Gmail account

Use `ahaseeb2002@gmail.com` for personal relationships or very informal outreach.

Good cases:

- Friends
- Family friends
- People who know the founder personally through that account
- Situations where the recipient already recognizes the Gmail address

Avoid using Gmail as the default for cold investor outreach unless there is a specific reason.

## Recommended operating workflow

1. Use `haseeb@orisen.ai` for most investor outreach.
2. Use `abdul.haseeb@uwaterloo.ca` for Waterloo-linked warm intros or ecosystem outreach.
3. Use `ahaseeb2002@gmail.com` only for personal-network conversations or backup.
4. Track every contact in `fundraising/investor-crm.md` regardless of which account was used.
5. Add a note in the CRM when a specific account was used and why.

## CRM note convention

When updating `fundraising/investor-crm.md`, use this format in the `Notes` column when email account choice matters:

```text
Sent from haseeb@orisen.ai.
```

or:

```text
Sent from abdul.haseeb@uwaterloo.ca because Waterloo connection is relevant.
```

or:

```text
Sent from ahaseeb2002@gmail.com because this is a personal relationship.
```
